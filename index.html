Privacy and Terms of Service

Personal identification information

We may collect personal identification information about Users from the Telegram Bot API and the Google Drive API. This includes Telegram user ID from the Telegram Bot API and Email address from the Google Drive API.


How we use collected information

  • Telegram User ID and Email Address: We collect, save, and use this information to operate the bot and provide our services effectively. Your identifiers are securely saved to facilitate your interactions with the bot. Your Email ID is used to differentiate between your linked Google Drive accounts.
  • Service Operations: Our services involve creating and modifying Google Drive files. We only access your data on your behalf when you instruct us to do so.
  • Log Files: We follow a standard procedure of using log files, which log users when they use the bot. This includes Telegram user ID, Telegram username, date and time, actions performed, and URLs/Telegram file identifiers, primarily when issues arise. The information collected is used for analyzing trends, administering the bot, and tracking errors.

Data Protection Mechanisms

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.


Retention of Data

Your data, including Telegram user ID and Email address, is stored only for as long as you use our services. Upon your request or when you log out, your data is permanently deleted from our database.


Data Deletion

When you choose to stop using our services or log out, all your data, including Telegram user ID and Email address, is immediately and permanently deleted from our servers.


Sharing your personal information

We do not sell, trade, or rent Users' personal identification information to others. We may use the data for promoting our own services, but no third party will be involved.

Changes to This Policy
